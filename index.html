ABOUT EESI

Executive Enforcement Services, Inc. (EESI) was born out of a desire by the owner, C.L. Green, to provide protective services to individuals and businesses in his community. Mr. Green began his career as a bouncer. As he continued to work at sporting events and various other venues, he began to put together a crew of his own, which led to the launching of EESI. C.L.'s vision and leadership abilities have allowed him to expand from primarily catering to the entertainment community, to providing community and subdivision patrols, concierge services, and providing security needs for corporate accounts.
